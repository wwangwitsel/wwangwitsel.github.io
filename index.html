Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Imperfect Information Learning Team at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP). I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Tokyo, where I was very fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Masashi Sugiyama and to work with Dr. Gang Niu. I received my M.Eng. degree from Southeast University, where I was also very fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Min-Ling Zhang. I received my B.Eng. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research interests lie in machine learning. Specifically, I am interested in data-centric machine learning, reliable machine learning, and LLM post-training.
