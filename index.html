Biography

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at the University of Tokyo, supervised by Prof. Masashi Sugiyama. I am also a Junior Research Associate at the Imperfect Information Learning Team, RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project. I received a M.Eng. degree from Southeast University under the supervision of Prof. Min-Ling Zhang. I received a B.Eng. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I worked as a machine learning engineer intern at Alibaba Group and DiDi.

My research interests are in algorithms and theories of machine learning. Specifically, I am mainly interested in reliable machine learning, data-centric machine learning, and applications in foundation models.

Feel free to email me if you are interested in talking with me.
